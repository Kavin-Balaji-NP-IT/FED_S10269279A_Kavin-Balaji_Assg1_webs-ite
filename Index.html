Beyblade is a thrilling franchise that revolves around high-energy battles using spinning tops called Beyblades.

Originating as a Japanese manga, it has expanded into an anime series, toys, and video games

Players compete in intense tournaments, strategizing with unique Beyblade designs and powerful moves.

With themes of friendship, rivalry, and determination, Beyblade captivates audiences of all ages

making it a beloved part of the anime and gaming community worldwide.

Spriggan, a Balance Type Cho-Z Layer Beyblade, features dual spin directions, burst-resistant invincible mode, and a treasure-guarding motif.

Cho-Z Valkyrie is an Attack Type Layer with a knight motif, three wings for high attack potential, metal design, and an anti-burst "Cho-Z Awakening System."

Cho-Z Achilles is a Balance Type Energy Layer with a forward-facing shield and armored design, symbolizing the Greek hero Achilles.
